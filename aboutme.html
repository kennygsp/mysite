    • About Me

    Technology has always fascinated me from a very young age dating back to my first gaming console, the Nintendo 64. Ever since then I've been hooked, rather than asking for toys as other kids I was asking for the latest computer hardware. At first it was a surface level interest of the technology but it would soon come to change with the impact of Minecraft. From there I started exploring how to host my own server so that me and my friends could play and eventually hosting a server that my entire middle school class would join daily. I would find myself staying up late constantly messing around with the plugins code (Java) and found a new love for technology, this new passion is what led me to study IT at Rutgers University in hopes of working in the Tech Industry.
