    Furever Home

    This project was my group's solution on how to decrease euthanization rates within animal shelters across the country by creating a nonprofit organization that creates a coalition between shelters to effectively manage the animals in the country by sharing and updating a database. This database would be used by our app/website to give top priority to feature local shelthers (using user location) with higher populations first than lower ones and moving the adoption process online to make it easier and less time consuming for customers.

    My contributions to the project: Cross-function process model - outlined the adoption process our org will use SWOT anaylsis - analysis of our strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats Magic Quadrant - analysis of who our competitors are and what category we fall in UI/UX design - low & high fidelity prototype of our website that included options to search for specific age, gender and breed, shelters near the user, and catalogs for all the animals that separate them by user specifications choice.
